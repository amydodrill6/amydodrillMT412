Why I love to read

Reading is a captivating journey that transports us to different worlds, expands our horizons, and deepens our understanding of the human experience. Through some of my favourite books like "Tomorrow and Tomorrow and Tomorrow," "A Little Life," and the "Thursday Murder Club" series, we are immersed in rich narratives that evoke a myriad of emotions and provoke thought-provoking reflections. Each page turned unveils new perspectives, characters to empathize with, and stories to cherish. From the intricate tapestry of human relationships depicted in "A Little Life" to the gripping mysteries unraveling in the "Thursday Murder Club" series, reading offers an escape into realms of imagination and intellect. It fosters empathy, enhances cognitive abilities, and ignites the imagination, making it an indispensable source of joy, enlightenment, and enrichment in our lives. The reason I feel this is so relevant to this project, is because I have learnt everything I know about communication skills from reading. These skills I hope to bring into my future career with me.
